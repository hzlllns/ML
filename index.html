Artist & Educator

Marissa Largo is a Toronto-based artist and educator. She is a graduate of York University's Fine Arts and Education programs and has a Master's degree in Art Education from Concordia University. Her works have been presented in several group exhibitions in Montreal and Toronto:
  • Pasalubong, Pabaon and Padala (2010)
  • Public Realm (2010)
  • Memory and Place (2009)
  • From the Pearl of the Orient to Uptown (2007)
  • Departures (2005)
  • Greater than One (2004)
  • MEQ: Misconceptions Elegantly Quenched (2004)
  • Additions (2004)
  • Smudge (2004)

She has had two solo exhibitions:
  • Small World (2003)
  • Neither Here Nor There (2003)
